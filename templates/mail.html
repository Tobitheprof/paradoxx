Welcome to Paradoxx

Hello there, I'm Olwatobi Ekundayo, 17 year old full stack web developer and data scientist. I am the author of the application paradoxx.

Paradoxx is a free tool that helps to make learning easier with the help of flashcards.

You see, most people find it quite difficult to just dive right into a concept head first and that seems to be the root cause of unproductive traits such as procrastination and laziness.

To counter this, I made research and found out that the best way to get a person hooked on something is to give them a summary of it. This method has proven itself to be the best based on the tests I carried out.

I began to wonder how I can use this simple logic to build a webapp and came up with Paradoxx. Something that conveys summarized information in the best possbile way seemed to be flashcards. Hence, the idea of digitized flash cards.

Paradoxx is all about digitizing flashcards to help you learn better.

Thanks for giving Paradoxx a chnace. I do hope you enjoy it
